## [Auto Instrumentation](https://github.com/Arize-ai/phoenix/blob/docs/docs/tracing/how-to-tracing/broken-reference/README.md)

Phoenix natively works with a variety of frameworks and SDKs across [Python](/phoenix/tracing/how-to-tracing#python) and [JavaScript](/phoenix/tracing/how-to-tracing#javascript) via OpenTelemetry auto-instrumentation. Phoenix can also be natively integrated with AI platforms such as [LangFlow](https://github.com/Arize-ai/phoenix/blob/docs/docs/tracing/how-to-tracing/broken-reference/README.md) and [LiteLLM proxy](https://github.com/Arize-ai/phoenix/blob/docs/docs/tracing/how-to-tracing/broken-reference/README.md).
